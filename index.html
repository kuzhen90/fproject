Daily Check-In

Peace of mind for families with aging parents

The Problem

77% of seniors want to age in place, but adult children worry constantly. Existing solutions feel like surveillance or are too complex for seniors to use.

The Solution

  • Senior taps one emoji daily: "How are you feeling?"
  • Optional voice notes and photos to share moments
  • Family dashboard shows status of all parents
  • Alerts if no check-in by a set time
  • Emergency button for urgent situations
  • Medication reminders with family visibility

Why It Wins

Dignity-first design. The senior initiates contact, not surveillance. 10-second daily interaction vs. complex health apps.

Sleep Coach AI

Your personal sleep coach that learns what works for YOU

The Problem

Calm and Headspace offer the same content to everyone. Sleep Cycle tracks but doesn't help you improve. Nothing learns YOUR patterns.

The Solution

  • AI-powered sleep assessment in Week 1
  • Personalized nightly recommendations
  • Track what you tried and how you slept
  • AI learns and adjusts to YOUR patterns
  • Insights like "You sleep 40min longer without screens after 9pm"
  • Progress dashboard showing improvement

Why It Wins

Personalization gap. Unlike one-size-fits-all meditation apps, this learns YOUR sleep patterns and adapts recommendations.

Market: $3.98B ‚Üí $17.5B (17.9% CAGR)

Why These Markets?

Strategic analysis based on comprehensive market research from consulting firms and industry reports.

Low Competition

Zero senior-focused apps in the top 100 health apps. Sleep market is crowded but lacks true personalization.

Personal Connection

Built from personal experience with aging parents and a partner with sleep issues. Authenticity matters.

High Willingness to Pay

Adult children pay for peace of mind. Sleep-deprived people are motivated buyers.

Low Capital Required

No hardware dependencies. MVP can be built by small team in 8 weeks. Self-service model reduces CAC.

B2B Expansion Path

Senior living facilities, insurance companies, and employers all need these solutions.

Subscription Revenue

44% of App Store revenue comes from subscriptions. Recurring revenue is investor-friendly.
