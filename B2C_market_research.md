# B2C Consumer App Market Research

**Analysis Date:** December 24, 2024

---

## Executive Summary

### Key Findings

- Global mobile app IAP revenue reached **$150 billion in 2024** (+13% YoY) - highest growth since 2021
- Non-gaming apps are the growth engine: **+25% YoY** vs gaming at +4%
- **AI apps** are a billion-dollar category, approaching **$1.3B in IAP revenue** in 2024
- Downloads declined for the first time (-1% YoY) while revenue and engagement grew - quality over quantity
- **Health & Fitness** apps expected to surpass **$4B revenue in 2025** (100% growth over 5 years)

### Market Size & Trajectory

| Metric | 2024 | 2025 Projection | Long-term |
|--------|------|-----------------|-----------|
| Global IAP Revenue | $150B | $170B+ | $780B+ by 2029 |
| Mobile Ad Spend | $402B | $447B | 56% of all digital ad spend |
| App Downloads | 149B | Stable | Quality focus |

## Emerging/Underserved Niches

### High-Opportunity Gaps

| Niche | Opportunity | Why Underserved |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Mental Health** | Growing demand post-pandemic | Stigma, quality concerns |
| **Senior Wellness** | 60% of seniors now use apps | Age-specific UX lacking |
| **Small Business Finance** | SMB owners need mobile-first tools | Enterprise focus by incumbents |
| **Localized Delivery** | Hyperlocal logistics | Hard to scale |
| **Pet Services** | Pet travel, health tracking | Fragmented market |
| **Niche Skill Learning** | No-code, specific software tools | Broad platforms dominate |

### Specific App Ideas with Market Potential

1. **AR Try-On for Niche Products**
   - Luxury watches, custom PCs, eyewear, sneakers
   - Start narrow, expand later

2. **Community Apps for Underserved Groups**
   - Specific hobbies, professions, or demographics
   - Network effects create moats

3. **Hybrid Content Platforms**
   - Combine multiple media types
   - Focus on underserved content categories

4. **Digital Wellness Tools**
   - Screen time management
   - Digital detox features
   - Smartphone addiction support

5. **Outdoor/Real-World Integration**
   - Gardening apps
   - Shared workspace management
   - Apps connecting digital to physical experiences

## Strategic Recommendations

### For B2C App Builders

1. **Build AI-Native**
   - AI is no longer optional; 72% of organizations adopted AI in 2024
   - ChatGPT's 52M monthly downloads shows consumer appetite

2. **Target High-Growth Verticals**
   - Health & Fitness (100% growth over 5 years)
   - Fintech (strong consumer demand for financial control)
   - AI productivity tools

3. **Consider Underserved Demographics**
   - Seniors (60% now use mobile apps)
   - Emerging market users (34% of new downloads)
   - Niche professional communities

4. **Subscription-First Monetization**
   - 44% of App Store revenue from subscriptions
   - Recurring revenue preferred by investors

5. **Cross-Platform Strategy**
   - iOS for revenue (higher ARPU)
   - Android for scale (110B+ downloads)
   - Windows for productivity/enterprise crossover

6. **Focus on Quality Over Quantity**
   - Downloads declining while revenue grows
   - Google's new 20-user testing requirement signals quality focus

### What to Avoid

- Weather apps (-34.63% decline)
- Generic lifestyle apps (-5.86%)
- News aggregators (-5.61%)
- Comics apps (-9.98%)
- Horizontal tools without AI differentiation
